About APEX

Making STEM accessible. Inspiring the next generation.

Who are we?

We are a team of students who believe STEM should not feel distant or out of reach. Through robotics, we challenge ourselves to grow as engineers, leaders, and problem-solvers. Beyond building robots, we want to bring STEM into our community and help younger students see that they belong in this field too.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to break barriers in STEM and make sure every student, regardless of background, prior access, or financial situation, has the opportunity to learn, create, and innovate through robotics.

Outreach

We have spent a lot of time trying to share what we love about robotics with others in our community. One of the most meaningful things we have done is bringing our robot to elementary school students at local math and English academies, as well as Brookhaven Elementary School. Seeing their reactions — how excited they get when they first see the robot move or try things themselves — reminds us why we started in the first place.

We also connected with FTC and VEX teams at Walter Johnson High School, where we shared our experiences and learned from each other. It was not just about showing our robot, but about building a stronger robotics community and helping others grow alongside us.

Outside of competitions, we try to support students more directly by offering low-cost or free tutoring for those who need it. We know not everyone has the same access to resources, so we want to do our part to make STEM feel more reachable and less intimidating.
