About Apex

Precision, purpose, and community.

Apex Dimension was built around a simple idea: talent is everywhere, but access is not. Through FIRST Robotics, we create opportunities for students to build technical skills, confidence, and a real sense of belonging in engineering.

Who we are

We are a student-led robotics team focused on design, programming, manufacturing, teamwork, and outreach. Every season, we push ourselves to create competitive robots while also expanding access to STEM for younger students and underserved communities.


Our team values curiosity, resilience, and impact. We do not just want to build better machines. We want to build a stronger future for the students around us.

Mission

Our mission is to make STEM accessible regardless of race, family background, prior exposure, or financial situation, and to create meaningful pathways for students to learn, build, and lead through robotics.

What drives us

We are not just building robots — we are building access, confidence, and opportunity. Every outreach demo, every conversation, and every student we inspire matters.

Outreach

STEM becomes real when students can touch it.

We bring robotics into our community through live demonstrations, mentorship, and direct student support.

What we do

We introduce students to robotics in a way that feels exciting, interactive, and personal. Our outreach is designed to make STEM feel less intimidating and more possible.

Elementary school robot demos

We brought our robot to local math and English academies and to Brookhaven Elementary School, giving younger students a hands-on first look at robotics in action.

FTC and VEX collaboration

We connected with FTC and VEX teams at Walter Johnson High School to share ideas, strengthen local robotics pathways, and support a more connected STEM community.

Tutoring and direct support

We offer low-cost or free tutoring to students who need extra support, because access to learning should never depend only on resources.
